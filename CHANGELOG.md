# Changelog

## 0.1.0 (2026-04-03)

Initial release.

- 10 tools (3 meta + 7 domain)
- Organic standards: Soil Association, OF&G, EU Baseline (Reg 2018/848)
- Permitted inputs: fertilisers, plant protection, feed additives with conditions and limits
- Conversion rules: 6 farm types with timelines, marketing, and support
- Cover crops: 12 species/mixes with N fixation, biomass, sowing windows
- Biodiversity guidance: 8 habitat types with BNG units, costs, grants
- Soil health: 8 indicators with targets, methods, management practices
- FTS5 full-text search across all topics
- Streamable HTTP + stdio transport
- Docker and npm distribution
