# Disclaimer

This MCP server provides organic farming and regenerative agriculture data for informational purposes only.

## Not Professional Advice

The data served by this tool does not constitute professional agricultural, agronomic, or certification advice. Organic standards, permitted inputs, conversion rules, and other data points are based on published UK and EU sources, but certification requirements vary by body. Always check with your certification body (Soil Association, OF&G, etc.) before making management decisions.

## Data Sources

All data is sourced from:

- **EU Regulation 2018/848** (retained in UK law) -- organic production and labelling
- **Soil Association Standards** -- additional requirements beyond EU baseline
- **OF&G Standards** -- Organic Farmers & Growers certification requirements
- **DEFRA** -- Environmental Land Management schemes, Biodiversity Net Gain guidance
- **AHDB** -- Cover crop and soil health research

All data from government sources is used under the [Open Government Licence v3](https://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/doc/open-government-licence/version/3/).

## Currency

Organic standards change. EU Regulation 2018/848 is periodically amended. Certification body standards are updated annually. This tool shows the data available at the time of its last ingestion run. Check `check_data_freshness` for the age of the data.
